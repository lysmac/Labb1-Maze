Welcome to the Maze game

The goal of this game is to make it through the maze. Simple, right? Beware of the hungry wolf though, who likes to lurk around the exit.

Instructions:

There is only four commands you need to know to move around the maze.

  • East
  • South
  • West

That's all. If you're up for the task, click the button below.
